About AUDI

Audi is a German car manufacturer that combines sophisticated design and modern technology. Its headquarters is located in the town of Ingoldstadt. In 1909, the history of this automotive giant begins, when Augustus Horch organized a car manufacturing company. In 1910, the first Audi-A car was launched. In 1965, the Volkswagen group bought a controlling stake in Audi. One of the large-scale developments of the company's specialists was the company four-wheel drive, which in 1982 equipped the Audi 80 quattro. The 90s for the company were filled with technological innovations. For the serial car A8, an aluminum case was made, for the A6 they created a variator with a metal belt. To date, the company Audi produces a whole range of cars of various classes.
