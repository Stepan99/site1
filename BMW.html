About BMW

BMW is a German company that produces premium-class cars and motorcycles. Its headquarters is located in the city of Munich, and the story begins in 1917, when Karl Rapp and Gustav Otto set up their own production plant for aircraft engines Bayerische Motoren Werke - BMW. For the expansion of the company in 1928, the company acquires automobile plants in Eisenach. In the same year, she buys a license to produce economical Dixi machines. In the 70s, the design department of BMW developed a digitally controlled engine, and in 1980 it introduces the ABS system for the first time. Since 1988, the production of the first German roadster, equipped with a 12-cylinder engine, begins. Today, the company produces high-quality, high-quality cars that are popular not only in their homeland, but throughout the world. Today, the cars of this concern have become a symbol of German quality and reliability.)
